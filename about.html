Our Vision

Our vision to be recognized as a high-class technological college supporting economic development of the state by preparing competent students to meet local and international workforce.

Our Mission

  • To provide effective teaching learning processes through internationally standardized and industry-cooperated curricula
  • To prepare students who are not only technically strong but also equipped with 21st century soft skills
  • To ensure that students are readily employable in industries, and socially responsible citizen
